about leads

Get alerts and or search for Requests made by potential buyers that match your selected keywords.

Set your Lead preference by creating a Lead Subscription

  • Set Subscription Title (Something that describes the type of leads gotten)
  • Pick a date range (The least date you want to receive leads/requests, and the maximum/farthest date.)
  • Enter keywords (These are words used to filter through our pool of requests. Be creative. Think of words users may use when making a request for a product/service)
  • Select a preferred subscription package These specifies the maximum number of leads you can get, and the amount required

NOTE:

  • Leads don't expire
  • A lead subscription can be edited
  • Your lead count is only counted when you view a lead/request
